Who are we?

180 Degrees Consulting is the world’s largest student-run consultancy organization for non-profits & social enterprises. We develop innovative, practical and sustainable solutions to the challenges non-profits in the area are facing.

180 Degrees Consulting Beijing is a brand-new addition to 180DC, founded in 2023 as the first branch in Mainland China.

Our services

How we can help your social enterprise.

Market Research

We conduct detailed market research and competitor analysis to help our clients gain an in-depth understanding of the sectoral landscape, identify opportunities and navigate challenges.

Financial Planning

We formulate funding, revenue generation and cost-cutting strategies thereby helping our clients build financially sustainable organisations.

Process Optimization

We identify key performance indicators and help our clients optimize business processes to maximize their operational efficiency.

Join one of our consulting projects

Students at one of the Beijing universities can join our association and be part of the 180DC experience. You will be assigned to a project together with other team members, where you will work together to creating lasting social impact for the organization.

Through strong connections in the consulting industry, both professionally and in student associations, 180DC Beijing is able to form strong connections with organizations from around the world. By partnering with other institutions, we provide our consultants with a diverse range of project proposals to work on.
